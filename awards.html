ENHYPEN AWARDS

Even though Enhypen debuted in 2020, they have built a good path together with their fans and with their albums they have been nominated to many awards. They have been nominated to 42 awards and have winned 30 of them, showing the talent and effort that they always put to their career.

Some of this are:

  • Golden Disk Award Rookie Artist Award 2021
  • MAMA Award for Worldwide Fans' Choice 2022, 2021
  • Mnet Asian Music Award to the New Best Masculine Artist 2021
  • Seoul Music Awards for Best New Artist 2021 · Border: Day One
  • Top Ten Award for the Best Musical Artist 2021
  • Seoul Music Awards Bonsang Awards 2022 · Border: Carnival
  • Golden Disk Awards: First Album Division 2023, 2022 · Manifesto: Day 1, Dimension: Dilemma
  • Seoul Music Awards Bonsang Awards 2022 · Border: Carnival

MUSIC BANK MC

Sunghoon began hosting “Music Bank” alongside IVE's Jang Won Young in October 2021 and the couple took home the Best Couple award at the 2021 KBS Entertainment Awards.On September 1, SPOTV News reported that Sunghoon’s final “Music Bank” broadcast as an MC would air the next day. Later, a representative from ENHYPEN's agency, BELIFT LAB, revealed, "Sunghoon will be leaving 'Music Bank' and the September 2 broadcast will be his last."

It is said that he wanted to focus on the promotions of his group ENHYPEN, which at the time was embarking on its first world tour in October with concerts in Seoul later that month. Sung-hoon was MC for 11 months
